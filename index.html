About me


I am currently a Ph.D. student in the School of Electronic & Information Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT), supervised by Prof. Qianhua He. Before that, I received the M.Eng. degrees in Information and Communication Engineering from Guangdong University of Technology (GDUT), China in 2017, supervised by Prof. Nian Cai.

My research interests include various aspects of Signal Analysis and Machine Intelligence. My recent research is focus on Computational Analysis of Eenvironmental Sound in real life scenarios.
